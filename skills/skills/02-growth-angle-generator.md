# Growth Angle Generator

## Purpose

Transform a structured brand brief into a clear, strategic TikTok Shop growth angle for cold outbound BD outreach.

This skill identifies:
- Why TikTok Shop fits the brand
- What growth path is most suitable
- What objections may arise
- How to frame value strategically

---

## Inputs

- Structured brand brief (from brand-brief-builder)
- Optional: category context (e.g., Jewelry, Fashion, Accessories)
- Optional: brand constraints (no live, no discounting, premium positioning, etc.)

---

## Outputs

- primary_growth_model (Affiliate-led / Ads-led / Hybrid / Live-led)
- supporting_growth_model
- strategic_fit_reasoning (3 bullets)
- objection_risks (2-3 bullets)
- mitigation_angles
- 30-60-90_day_activation_outline
- pitch_positioning_summary (2-3 sentences usable in outreach)

---

## Step-by-Step Behavior

1. Review brand positioning and product signals.
2. Identify which TikTok Shop lever aligns best:
   - Creator discovery
   - Affiliate performance
   - Paid amplification
   - Live commerce
3. Select ONE primary model and ONE supporting model.
4. Generate 3 brand-specific strategic fit reasons.
5. Anticipate likely objections (brand safety, discount dilution, return rates, etc.).
6. Provide mitigation framing.
7. Outline a realistic 30-60-90 day rollout plan.
8. Produce a short pitch positioning summary usable in email.

---

## Constraints

- Do not fabricate platform performance numbers.
- Do not over-promise GMV.
- Avoid generic claims like "TikTok is growing fast."
- Tailor strategy to brand positioning (e.g., luxury brands should avoid heavy discount framing).
- If insufficient information exists, mark uncertainty clearly.

---

## Failure Modes

- Defaulting to generic "affiliate-led growth" without justification
- Overusing discount strategy for premium brands
- Generating unrealistic rollout timelines
- Not accounting for brand positioning

If brand positioning is Unknown, provide conditional recommendations.
